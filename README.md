# 💫 About Me:
The Cockroach Janta Party Assam (CJP) is an <br>Indian (Assam) satirical political movement <br> founded on 13 June 2026 by <br> Dipen Brahma, <br>
a political communications strategist who<br>formerly worked with the Aam Aadmi Party.<br>It was created in response to remarks made <br>on 15 May 2026 by Surya Kant, the<br>Chief Justice of India, in which he called<br>some activists and unemployed youth<br>"cockroaches" and "parasites of society",<br>sparking immediate national outrage.<br><br>The movement has also engaged in<br>offline activities, with volunteers <br>participating in protests and clean-up <br>drives dressed in cockroach costumes.<br>Though the movement is not registered <br>as a political party with the <br>Election Commission of India, <br>it campaigns against broader societal, <br>economic, <br>and political issues affecting the Indian youth.
